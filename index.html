About Us

Hamro Vaidya, is over a Decade old company working in field of Health Informatics.Hamro Vaidya has developed SISH (Software for Integrated Services in Hospital), a popular EMR (Electronic Medical Record) with inbuilt modules of CDSS (Clinical decision support system), CPOE(Computerized Physician Order Entry) and data mining. In addition to these, SISH also specializes in Statistical Programme for Academic Research and Health System Integration to financial technology.

Here at Hamro Vaidya, our dedicated team is constantly in the process of improving and innovating Hospital Information System (HIS). We study medical workflow, understand the causes of health problems and develop digital methods to mitigate those problems. Keeping in mind the lack of fundamental features in HIS softwares that are available in the market, we have developed and tested a complete and comprehensive Software.Hamro Vaidya is purely made in Nepal that is insurance compatible and in-line with Policy Directives of Nepal Government.

Hamro Vaidya installed its first CDSS modules in 2008 in United Mission Hospital, Palpa. Today SISH has been implemented in over two dozens locations including some of the prestigious institutions like Patan Academy of Health Sciences, Paropkar Maternity & Women Hospital, Nepal Police Hospital and Nepal Orthopaedic Hospital.

We are here to develop evidence based medical education and practice by developing a research culture through Hamro vaidya.

We will transform the way health information is managed and used.
